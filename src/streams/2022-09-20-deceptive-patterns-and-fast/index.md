[Todd Libby](https://twitter.com/toddlibby), noted lobster roll connoisseur, rejoins the stream, this time to dive into the work he's been preparing for [WCAG 3](https://www.w3.org/TR/wcag-3.0/). Join us to learn about the latest research on the impact deceptive patterns can have on accessibility and user experience, as well as recent developments on the [Framework for Accessible Specification of Technologies (FAST)](https://raw.githack.com/w3c/fast/restructure-functional-and-user-needs/index.html)!
